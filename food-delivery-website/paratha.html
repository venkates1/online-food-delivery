Aloo Paratha
The most popular paratha from Punjab where spicy potato stuffing is used.Piping-hot crisp wheat flatbreads stuffed with spiced, faintly sour mashed potatoes stuffing and relished with some tangy sour mango pickle. what is not to love about aloo paratha.
30
Plain Paratha
Crisp as well as soft unleavened whole wheat flat indian bread. plain paratha are popular breakfast or tiffin recipe that can be served with pickle or any side veggie dish.
20
Paneer Paratha
Whole wheat flat bread stuffed with crumbled, spiced cottage cheese and then shallow fried with oil. This is punjabi paneer paratha.
40
Methi Paratha
these enugreek flatbreads are a healthy alternative to plain parathas and packed with more flavors and nutrition.
20

Gobi Paratha
Gobi paratha is often made for breakfast in punjabi homes as well as served in restaurants and punjabi dhabas. in punjabi homes, while making gobi paratha, the gobi is not sautéed or cooked before. the grated gobi is directly added as a stuffing.
35
Onion Paratha
For all onion lovers these onion parathas are a must to have. easy to prepare and they also make a good tiffin box snack or lunch.
30
Lacchha Paratha
Lachedar paratha is a popular paratha recipe from north india. its also called as paratwala paratha. in punjabi “parat” means layer and layers can be seen on the paratha when it gets cooked. you can serve the lachha paratha hot with any vegetable curry.
30
Mooli Paratha
Delicious punjabi parathas stuffed with spiced grated radish. mooli parathas are very filling as well as delicious to eat. they are usually served as breakfast in punjabi homes.
110

Cabbage Paratha
Parathas stuffed with a spiced cabbage stuffing. these no onion no garlic cabbage parathas make for a healthy breakfast as well as makes for a good tiffin box brunch or lunch.
40
Cheese Paratha
Easy to prepare delicious parathas with a spiced cheese stuffing. these cheese parathas are best served hot. so not advisable for them to be a tiffin box snack.
50
Dal Paratha
parathas stuffed with a spiced chana dal or bengal gram mixture. this is a filling paratha because of the addition of chana dal. the paratha best goes with green chili pickle or mango pickle, yogurt and white butter.
30
Masala Paratha
Masala paratha is a variation of plain paratha with some spices added in it. masala paratha are spiced but not spicy or hot. though you can increase the red chili powder and garam masala powder to increase the spiciness in the parathas.
25
